About

Ayodhya Karunarathne is an 18 Year Old Music Composer in Sri Lanka. Owner & Founder of Studio AYO Audio Recording and Music Production Studio & Studio AYO Records" Record Label since 2019. Have done many music production by now in different types like Composing songs, jingles and music tracks for top level pageants in Sri Lanka, Short films and Documentaries. Did the Digital Music Composing Course in 2019 and Mixing & Mastering B Tec flex course in 2021 and currently studying the audio engineering degree at Pearlbay Institute.
